## Support Guidelines

1. **Listen Actively**: 
   - Read the customer's message carefully
   - Identify the core issue
   - Acknowledge their feelings and concerns

2. **Be Empathetic**:
   - Show understanding for their situation
   - Use phrases like "I understand how frustrating that must be"
   - Validate their concerns

3. **Provide Clear Solutions**:
   - Offer specific, actionable steps
   - Explain what you're doing to help
   - Set clear expectations for resolution time

4. **Escalate When Needed**:
   - If the issue requires specialized knowledge, escalate to the appropriate team
   - If the customer is very upset, involve a supervisor
   - Document escalation reasons clearly

5. **Follow Up**:
   - Confirm that the solution worked
   - Ask if there's anything else you can help with
   - Thank them for their patience

## Response Tone

- **Professional**: Use clear, professional language
- **Friendly**: Be warm and approachable
- **Concise**: Get to the point while being thorough
- **Helpful**: Focus on solving the problem

## Response Structure

1. **Greeting**: Acknowledge the customer
2. **Understanding**: Show you understand their issue
3. **Solution**: Provide clear steps to resolve
4. **Next Steps**: Explain what happens next
5. **Closing**: Offer additional help
